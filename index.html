About me

My name is Aly Vlassis and I am currently a junior at James Madison University. I am studying Media Arts and Design with a concentration in Interactive Design. This year I have worked with different programs including java, open processing, and bootstrap.

Project 1 - Abstract Art

The abstracts art project was our first project in open processing. The goal was to create our own version of a painting by Piet Mondrian in code. I used P5.js and open processing to create this sketch.

Project 2 - Animation

The second project was a sketch that included an animation that responded to the users mouse. For my project I chose to do an airplane that would follow your mouse as you moved it around the screen. Another element that I added is when you click down on your mouse it will change to night time.

View Project 2

Project 3

For the third project in SMAD 308, we did a portrait of any character or person we wanted. I chose to do my sketch of a penguin. In this project, we had to use the Shape functions that is in the P5.js and open processing code. There was a lot of trial and error for these shapes to find the ones that fit and looked the best.

View Project 3

Project 4

Project 4 we had to create a sketch that visualizes time in a creative way using P5.js and open processing. Our sketch has to illustrate the passing of time through a image we created. I created a sunset on the beach to visualize the passing of time by the changing of the sky throuhout the day. We had to use the hour(), minute(), second() functions to create the sketch and use if statements and custom functions. Throughout the day, depending on the time someone looks at my sketch the color of the sky will be a different color.

View Project 4

Project 5

The fifth project in SMAD 308 was a to create a data visualization of our choosing. We had to load our data from a JSON or CSV file. We could choose to take data from whatever we wanted. I choose to count the different color t-shirts I own.
